# N21 Hypotheses

N21 tests whether the N20 becoming-primitive contract can produce bounded
source-backed evidence for withdrawal resistance and naturalization depth.

Hypotheses:

```text
A - Withdrawal Resistance Under Declared Support Reduction
B - Naturalization Depth After Probe Or Scaffold Removal
C - Claim Boundary And Producer-Residue Control
```

All hypotheses remain artifact-level and LGRC-visible. None may be interpreted
as agency, native support, selfhood, identity acceptance, sentience, or Phase 8
implementation.

Cross-hypothesis dependency:

```text
Hypothesis A or B may pass only if Hypothesis C also passes.
If Hypothesis C fails, positive WR/ND rows are demoted or blocked regardless
of their local margins.
```
