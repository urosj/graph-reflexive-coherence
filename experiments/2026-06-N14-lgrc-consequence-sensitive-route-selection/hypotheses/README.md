# N14 Hypotheses

N14 hypotheses are specific to consequence-sensitive route selection. They are
not a generic continuation of N13 support-seeking regulation.

The experiment asks whether route selection can depend on source-backed,
pre-selection downstream effect records over support, memory, or regulation,
while blocking hidden outcome labels, post-hoc scoring, intention relabels,
agency relabels, and native-support relabels.

Hypothesis files:

- `hypothesis_a_pre_selection_consequence_records.md`
- `hypothesis_b_rank_sensitive_route_selection.md`
- `hypothesis_c_intention_and_agency_boundary.md`
