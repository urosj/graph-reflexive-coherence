from the target project and imports `N05-N28` as capability supply, not as a
chronological re-proof.

```text

  N05-N28 artifacts, closeouts, reviews, and source inventories

ecology target side:
  RC-agentic-ecology papers and shared-medium engineering specification

agency interpretation side:
  Agency of Becoming and Arc of Becoming diagnostic method
```

```text

```

