# N16 Hypotheses

N16 hypotheses are specific to artifact-level basin-boundary stability
requirements across lineage-derived boundary-state and operational
challenge-class interaction cells.
They are not a generic continuation of N15 endogenous proxy formation and they
are not a full self/environment taxonomy.

The experiment asks whether internal support-relevant state and external
resource or perturbation state can remain separable across boundary-state
sweeps, challenge-class sweeps, and selected interaction probes while blocking
externally supplied boundary labels, post-hoc labels, hidden external-state
injection, identity-acceptance relabels, selfhood/personhood relabels,
semantic-goal-ownership relabels, agency relabels, and native-support relabels.
