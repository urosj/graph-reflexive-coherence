# Hypothesis A - Coherence-Only Derived Causal Decay

## Statement

Ordinary source-current LGRC9V3 coherence and causal continuation dynamics can
produce a route-local relation that forms, persists after forming activity
stops, weakens under system-internal progression, and causally changes a later
local readout without adding an independently evolving decay, age, memory, or
susceptibility state.

## Required Support

- D0a representation is native or an exact non-independent projection.
- Attributable activity changes complete route-local C/J_C organization.
- Route mass, route organization, and causal mediation are defined by separate
  contracts and measured independently.
- Forming activity stops before the weakening window.
- The relation persists and then weakens under ordinary admitted dynamics.
- A later local transport/readout depends on the changed coherence mediator.
- Mediator intervention changes or removes that later effect while route mass
  and other continuation state are matched, or the result is explicitly
  limited to bounded partial mediation.
- Any D0-R export result closes its signed route-boundary continuity residual
  and shows that ordinary post-formation evolution, not an added export policy,
  generated the flux.
- No load-bearing producer updates post-formation state or schedules decay.
- Conservation, restoration identity v2 where required, replay, and controls
  pass.

## Failure Conditions

- Only a label, report row, cached score, or finite-window count changes.
- Only node-scalar coherence is matched while omitted spatial/flux state differs.
- A persistent `C_slow`, age, route memory, or susceptibility scalar is invented.
- A producer performs relaxation or future-affecting post-formation updates.
- The effect disappears immediately with forming current and is only D0c.
- The route relation changes but does not mediate later local transport.
- Lower route mass is used as proof of weakened organization or causal mediation.
- An added export policy is relabeled as ordinary D0-R because it conserves coherence.

## Claim Ceiling

```text
bounded coherence-only derived causal decay relation
```

Not memory, learning, trail intelligence, stigmergy, communication, or agency.
