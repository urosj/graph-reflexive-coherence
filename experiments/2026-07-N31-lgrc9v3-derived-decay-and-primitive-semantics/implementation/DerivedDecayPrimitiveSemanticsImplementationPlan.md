# N31 Derived Decay And Primitive Semantics Implementation Plan

## Purpose

Determine whether LGRC9V3 can express a causal, route-local,
durable-yet-weakening relation through ordinary coherence dynamics, or whether
the required phenomenon belongs to a derived observable, effective closure,
missing runtime surface, or distinct added mechanism.

N31 is selected by an exact Agentic Ecology demand after N30. It is not an
ecology implementation and does not treat that demand as evidence.

## Direction

N31 asks a semantic and executable question before an implementation question:

```text
what changes?
what carries the change?
did route mass change, did route organization change, or both?
what advances it?
where does conserved coherence go?
what later operation encounters the changed organization?
does intervention establish causal mediation rather than correlation?
is it derived or independently causal?
```

The experiment must not optimize a generic decay factor until one candidate
looks useful. It must preserve the distinctions among D0a, D0b, D0c, release
attenuation, conserved leakage, and susceptibility relaxation.

### D0-R/B-R Export-Ownership Refinement

The export interpretation refines D0 and B without adding a primary semantic
class:

```text

  ordinary admitted evolution weakens route organization

D0-R:
  D0 realized through conservative net export from a registered route region

  Candidate B realized through an added field-specific export policy
```

The causal owner is decisive. Conservation is required in both lanes, but it
does not promote an added policy to D0-R. B-R is only a bounded operational
analogue of D0-R when a separate registered bridge supports that relation.

## Local Ladders

### Decay-Relation Ladder

```text
DR0 = no source-current evidence
DR1 = attributable route-local state/relation formed
DR2 = relation persists after formation stops
DR3 = relation weakens under system-internal progression
DR4 = later local readout causally depends on the mediator
DR5 = intervention, replay, restoration, invariants, and controls pass
DR6 = bounded semantics and downstream contract are reusable
```

Rung rules:

- D0c cannot exceed DR1 unless persistence is independently demonstrated.
- D0b may reach DR3 as an observable but cannot reach DR4 without mediation.
- A may reach an expression-attenuation DR5 classification without becoming
  field-state decay.
- B may reach DR5 only with explicit destination and complete conservation.
- C may reach DR5 only with explicit added-state/closure status and complete
  restoration.
- DR6 is classification/contract readiness, not a universal decay law.

## Iteration Plan

### Iteration 1 - Source Inventory And Authority Admission

Build a source-only inventory over the exact RCAE demand, theory/substrate
sources, graph runtime/spec/test sources, and N08/N22/N30 evidence. Record
source roles, digests, consumption rules, existing capability, missing
surfaces, and claim boundaries. Open no positive evidence.

### Iteration 2 - Semantic, Representation, And Control Schema Freeze

Freeze taxonomy, ladders, required fields, D0 representation/projection
schema, normalized route-mass/organization/mediation contracts, D0-R/B-R
ownership and bridge fields, internal-time and invariant schemas, candidate topology schema,
restoration/reconstruction policy, control statuses, demotion precedence, and
return manifest schema. Open no positive evidence.

### Iteration 3 - Active Nulls And Failure Baselines

Instantiate false-positive paths. Active-null rows are schema/control fixtures,
not positive source-current evidence. They must fail closed before admission.

### Iteration 4 - D0a Representation Gate

Audit whether source-current LGRC9V3 can represent and intervene on the claimed
route-local slow coherence organization separately from route mass, boundary
flux, and later causal mediation. Freeze one status before positive execution.
Close D0a honestly if representation is lossy or missing.

### Iteration 5 - D0c Instantaneous Geometry Comparator

Measure current-state/flux-derived route geometry during forming current and
immediately after withdrawal. Establish the lower comparator without durable
aftereffect language.

### Iteration 6 - D0b Finite-Window Derived Relation

Construct an exact finite-window relation from admitted packet/flux history.
Test fading, cache removal/recomputation, restoration, and diagnostic
disconnection from transport. Observable-only evidence stays below DR4.

### Iteration 7 - D0a Source-Current Causal Probe

Run only if Iteration 4 admits native or exact representation. Test formation,
persistence, autonomous weakening, local dependence, mediator intervention,
complete-state equality, and strict D0 producer limits. Retain mass,
organization, and mediation results independently; classify ordinary export,
internal reorganization, slow-mode relaxation, or another bounded D0 subtype.

### Iteration 8 - D0 Replay, Controls, And Classification

Apply artifact replay, snapshot/load, reset-aware identity, duplicate replay,
branch continuation, invariant audits, and D0 controls. Classify D0a/D0b/D0c
separately, preserve D0-R as one D0 subtype, and decide whether
added-mechanism work is justified.

### Iteration 9 - Added-Mechanism Admission

If D0 is insufficient, freeze candidate-specific fixtures, equations, phases,
clocks, invariants, topology, and controls for A, B, and/or C. Record why each
candidate is executed, classified without execution, or inapplicable.

### Iteration 9-A - Release-Efficacy Attenuation Probe

Test registered age/phase effects on newly created packet amount while
excluding in-flight attenuation. Classify expression attenuation separately.

### Iteration 9-B - Conserved Leakage Probe

Test bounded source emission into an explicit destination. Audit complete
conservation and distinguish B-R policy ownership from ordinary D0-R flux.
Bound emitted amount by available source excess and exclude receiver state from
the later read path during the registered probe window.

### Iteration 9-C - Susceptibility Relaxation Probe

Test route-local conductance/susceptibility relaxation only under declared
producer/closure status. Preserve independent-state and naturalization debt.

### Iteration 10 - Added-Mechanism Replay And Controls

Apply candidate-specific intervention, replay, restoration, accounting,
hidden-state, topology, and producer/native controls.

### Iteration 11 - Comparative Semantic And Native-Admission Classification

Compare semantic fit, invariant closure, local causality, representation,
restoration, hidden state, producer residue, transfer scope, and authority.
Compare D0-R and B-R without presuming equivalence; record bridge status. Do not
rank nonequivalent mechanisms by raw effect magnitude.

### Iteration 12 - Closeout And RCAE Return Bundle

Produce the exact machine-readable return manifest and reader report. Record
DR and N31-C rungs, source identities, dispositions, reconstruction, claim
ceiling, normalized mass/organization/mediation contracts, D0-R/B-R ownership
and bridge status, blocked relabels, `src_diff_empty`, and P2-I3 recommendation.

N31-C6 requires return completeness, not a positive native primitive.

## Acceptance Boundary

N31 is closeout-ready when source authorities are exact, candidate meanings
remain distinct, D0 representation is honest, executed rows are source-current,
conservation/time/locality/restoration are explicit, controls fail closed,
producer/native/closure/extension status is visible, `src_diff_empty` is true,
selection or non-selection is reasoned, and the RCAE return is reconstructable.

## Claim Boundary

N31 can support a bounded graph-side decay relation, observable, primitive,
closure, extension requirement, or non-selection. It cannot establish the
RCAE trail/stigmergic field, ecology coordination, communication, memory,
learning, agency, selfhood, sentience, or organism/life.
