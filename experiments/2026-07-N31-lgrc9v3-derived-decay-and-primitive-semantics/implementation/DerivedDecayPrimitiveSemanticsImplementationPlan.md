# N31 Derived Decay And Primitive Semantics Implementation Plan

## Purpose

Determine whether LGRC9V3 can express a causal, route-local,
durable-yet-weakening relation through ordinary coherence dynamics, or whether
the required phenomenon belongs to a derived observable, effective closure,
missing runtime surface, or distinct added mechanism.

N31 is selected by an exact Agentic Ecology demand after N30. It is not an
ecology implementation and does not treat that demand as evidence.

## Direction

N31 asks a semantic and executable question before an implementation question:

```text
what changes?
what carries the change?
did route mass change, did route organization change, or both?
what advances it?
where does conserved coherence go?
what later operation encounters the changed organization?
does intervention establish causal mediation rather than correlation?
is it derived or independently causal?
```

The experiment must not optimize a generic decay factor until one candidate
looks useful. It must preserve the distinctions among D0a, D0b, D0c, release
attenuation, conserved leakage, and susceptibility relaxation.

### D0-R/B-R Export-Ownership Refinement

The export interpretation refines D0 and B without adding a primary semantic
class:

```text
D0:
  ordinary admitted evolution weakens route organization

D0-R:
  D0 realized through conservative net export from a registered route region

B-R:
  Candidate B realized through an added field-specific export policy
```

The causal owner is decisive. Conservation is required in both lanes, but it
does not promote an added policy to D0-R. B-R is only a bounded operational
analogue of D0-R when a separate registered bridge supports that relation.

### D0 Organization-Domain Breadth

D0a route organization may be spatial, geometric, functional, or temporal.
This admits three bounded variants without adding candidate classes:

```text
geometric shallowing:
  organization changes inside a registered support, possibly at constant mass;
  D0c remains the instantaneous comparator and mediation remains required

causal desynchronization:
  relative timing organization derived exactly from admitted proper-time,
  event, queue, or packet state weakens under native progression

causal dispersion:
  conserved packet coherence broadens across arrival time and native local
  processing later depends on that temporal distribution
```

These are not admitted by label. A new phase, resonance, eligibility, or
coincidence-window state is an effective closure, runtime extension, or
expression policy rather than coherence-only D0. A widening arrival histogram
is D0b unless the native temporal organization mediates a later local readout;
propagation of the original forming packets alone does not establish a
persistent route relation. Phase slip with recurrence is modulation, not
monotonic decay. Iteration 1 must classify the relevant timing and delay
surfaces as executable native behavior, exact projection, specified-but-missing
surface, or added closure before a positive temporal lane can run.

Rung rules:

- D0c cannot exceed DR1 unless persistence is independently demonstrated.
- D0b may reach DR3 as an observable but cannot reach DR4 without mediation.
- A may reach an expression-attenuation DR5 classification without becoming
  field-state decay.
- B may reach DR5 only with explicit destination and complete conservation.
- C may reach DR5 only with explicit added-state/closure status and complete
  restoration.
- DR6 is classification/contract readiness, not a universal decay law.

### Iteration 3 - Active Nulls And Failure Baselines

### Iteration 4 - D0a Representation Gate

Audit whether source-current LGRC9V3 can represent and intervene on the claimed

### Iteration 5 - D0c Instantaneous Geometry Comparator

Measure current-state/flux-derived route geometry during forming current and
immediately after withdrawal. Establish the lower comparator without durable
aftereffect language. Use a balanced native packet cycle so route mass and
anchor-relative C coordinates remain matched while the in-flight packet-current
component is present, then exhaust that forming cycle through native arrivals.
Treat historical arrived-packet records as context rather than active current,
and cap a nonpersistent D0c row at DR1. Record both registered-cycle and
canonical native-edge sign conventions plus node-current divergence. Separate
formation-input stop from forming-carrier exhaustion. Resolve each I3 control
through semantic comparability and regenerate it for the exact I5 candidate
when authority, organization domain, carrier, or continuation state differs.

### Iteration 6 - D0b Finite-Window Derived Relation

Construct an exact finite-window relation from admitted packet/flux history.
Test fading, cache removal/recomputation, restoration, and diagnostic
disconnection from transport. Observable-only evidence stays below DR4.

### Iteration 7 - D0a Source-Current Causal Probe

Run only if Iteration 4 admits native or exact representation. Test formation,
persistence, autonomous weakening, local dependence, mediator intervention,
complete-state equality, and strict D0 producer limits. Retain mass,

weakening order. The route-mass window must count departure/in-flight mass,

### Iteration 8 - D0 Replay, Controls, And Classification

branch continuation, invariant audits, and D0 controls. Classify D0a/D0b/D0c
separately, preserve D0-R as one D0 subtype, and decide whether
