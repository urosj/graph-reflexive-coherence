The post-N30 RCAE demand pass selected decay semantics as the first downward
discovery question. N31 separates native route state, fading observables,
conservative coherence redistribution, release attenuation, and susceptibility
relaxation instead of treating all of them as one decay coefficient.

N31 closes with:

```text
native D0a = DR2 formation and persistence; autonomous weakening unsupported
exact-derived D0b = DR3 fading observable without causal mediation
instantaneous D0c = DR1 comparator
A = producer-mediated DR5 expression-attenuation boundary
B-R = producer-mediated DR5 + DR6 contract-only
C.2 = relation DR2 + producer DR5 + native DR0 + DR6 contract-only
```

The two issued contracts are conditional, not ranked:

```text
B-R:
  conservative coherence redistribution
  attributable local route-mass and route-contrast weakening
  explicit destination
  producer-owned export lifecycle
  organization transfer to destination unsupported

C.2:
  activity-indexed susceptibility / effective-geometry relaxation
  exact native packet-history functional
  producer-owned constitutive insertion
```

`DR6_contract_only` means reusable, testable semantics and an acceptance
contract. It does not mean cross-context execution, native implementation,
ecology evidence, provider adoption, or a general decay law.

RCAE may re-admit either exact provider contract, not N31's positive evidence.
Its admission record must bind the N31 closeout digest and selected contract
path, file hash, internal digest, candidate, semantics, authority ceiling,
rungs, debt, controls, and forbidden claims. RCAE must then generate fresh
ecology-side evidence.

B-R and C.2 may be combined only as a new composition candidate. The combined
effect inherits neither individual `DR5` result and needs separate attribution
and controls for redistribution, susceptibility modification, and interaction.
