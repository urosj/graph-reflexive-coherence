# Hypothesis B - Replay / Stress Capacity Attribution

N28 can show that a source-current generative/extractive candidate remains
classifiable under replay, attribution controls, and bounded stress or
variant probes.

Expected support requires:

```text
artifact replay passed
snapshot/load replay passed
duplicate replay passed
focal basin remains above support/coherence floors
neighbor capacity metrics remain source-current
neighbor support and boundary integrity remain distinguishable
capacity attribution does not depend on hidden producer policy
merge/leakage controls fail closed
extractive contrast rows are classified distinctly
competitive persistence rows are not promoted to generative rows
stress variants do not erase the claimed capacity relation
```

Failure conditions:

```text
replay preserves focal basin only
capacity improvement disappears under replay
neighbor effect is reconstructed from report labels
stress reveals focal persistence drains neighbor support
competitive persistence is relabeled as generative persistence
extractive flattening is ignored
same N27 transfer artifact is reused as N28 evidence without new N28 traces
```

Hypothesis B supports only bounded artifact-level capacity attribution if
Hypothesis C also passes.
