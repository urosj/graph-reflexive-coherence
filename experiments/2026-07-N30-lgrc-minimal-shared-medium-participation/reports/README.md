# N30 Reports

N30 iteration reports will be stored here.

## Iteration Reports

- `n30_source_inventory_i1.md`: readable source inventory, consumption rules,
  source pinning, and evidence-closed claim boundary.
- `n30_schema_control_freeze_i2.md`: readable ladder/schema/control freeze for
  later participant, medium-surface, trace, and eligibility-dependency probes.
- `n30_active_nulls_i3.md`: readable active-null matrix showing which
  false-positive path, gate, rung, and hypothesis each null blocks.
- `n30_participant_admissibility_i4.md`: participant-only I4 report with the
  P2 carrier recognizability result, geometric interpretation, artifact list,
  and claim boundary.
- `n30_participant_admissibility_i4a.md`: participant-only strengthening report
  for a second P2 carrier under the N27 topology/fixture variant, including the
  geometric difference from I4 and the unchanged claim boundary.
- `n30_participant_boundary_support_i4b.md`: participant-only stress report
  that records I4 as boundary-limited and the I4-A carrier as a bounded P4
  participant candidate, with no medium-relation claim opened.
