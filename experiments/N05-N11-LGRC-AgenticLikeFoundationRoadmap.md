# N05-N11 LGRC Agentic-Like Foundation Roadmap

This roadmap records the planned cross-experiment arc from native LGRC
coherence circuits toward agentic-like dynamics candidates. It is not itself
evidence and does not promote claims. Each experiment must still produce its
own artifacts, controls, validators, and closeout.

This arc is ACO-inspired in structure, but it is not a claim that LGRC
communication must be ant-colony-like. The ant-colony analogy is used here as
a concrete scaffold because it naturally separates carrier waves, route choice,
identity anchors, trail/memory, regulation, and later integration. Other native
LGRC communication modes may become clearer after the substrate evidence from
N05 and later experiments is available.

The intended outcome is a native substrate foundation for:

```text
native_lgrc_agentic_like_dynamics_candidate
```

That label remains blocked until choice, identity, memory, regulation,
recovery, and artifact-only replay are separately validated without hidden
experiment-side steering.

## Methodological Sources

N05-N11 also use the Arc of Becoming methodology. These sources are referenced
by title only because they live outside this repository:

```text
Classification of Becoming
Interrogation of Becoming
Naturalization of Becoming
Cultivation of Becoming
```

They are essential guardrails for the unknown territory from N07 onward.

```text
Classification of Becoming:
    first ask what property appeared; preserve non-promoting results as
    observation tags or becoming classes only when they are reusable,
    generative, and coherence-preserving.

Interrogation of Becoming:
    treat probes as bounded questions, not proof. Record which boundary was
    crossed: eligibility, event, substrate consequence, structured consequence,
    source specificity, recurrence, or natural regime expression.

Naturalization of Becoming:
    distinguish probe-exposed capacity from native regime expression. Ask
    whether the system can generate the support function that the observer or
    producer previously supplied.

Cultivation of Becoming:
    organize the arc as orient, observe, classify, probe, withdraw,
    naturalize, and integrate. Cultivate the missing function, not the proxy.
```

These sources do not promote claims. They constrain interpretation. Every
N07-N11 positive result should record whether it is a local observation tag,
reusable becoming class, probe-supported capacity, support-dependent
expression, endogenous precondition candidate, or native regime expression.

## Composition Logic

The arc is ordered to remove hidden scaffolding one layer at a time:

```text
producer-mediated evidence
-> runtime-visible triggers
-> native route choice
-> identity invariance
-> memory-shaped choice
-> goal-proxy regulation
-> identity-continuous cross-context integration
```

N05 starts the arc because later behavior needs stable coherence-circuit
primitives. N06 and N07 should precede ACO-like work so labels such as "nest",
"food", and "trail" are not only fixture names. N08 separates trail or
affordance memory from colony-like interpretation. N09 separates regulation
from intention. N10 is where the pieces may first be composed into a bounded
agentic-like integration candidate. N11 is reserved for broader/general
agentic-like integration after the bounded composition is artifact-valid and
claim-clean. Locomotion-like dynamics is now later dependent work, not the N11
foundation step, because it should consume rather than precede bounded and
general integration evidence.

N06 specifically asks whether a selected route can be explained by serialized
runtime-visible context or affordance evidence. Pheromone or trail following is
not N06; that belongs to N08 because it requires route-history or memory
surfaces. Native route arbitration is also not sufficient by itself. It becomes
N06 evidence only when artifacts reconstruct a complete candidate set,
context relation, selected route, rejected routes, and controls without hidden
fixture preferences or experiment-side if/else.

N07 specifically asks whether a recurrent support area is an RC/GRC/LGRC
identity attractor, not merely a node label, selected route, movement trace, or
support mask. It must map the identity gates from the full theory stack into
LGRC observables:

```text
RC identity basin definition
-> GRC directed-flux basin / sink attraction domain
-> GRC-v3 basin-attribute bundle
-> GRC9V3 nine-port basin chart / spark-refinement boundary
-> LGRC proper-time and lineage-current persistence
-> pulse-substrate identity-carrier taxonomy

support area
-> stability / local well evidence
-> attractivity / neighborhood flux convergence
-> invariance across cycles, perturbation, or lineage
-> reflexive closure / re-entry maintains basin evidence
-> coherence compatibility
-> artifact-only identity replay
```

N07 may use N06 route-choice artifacts as context, but route choice does not
become identity evidence unless the selected or recurrent basin independently
passes support, stability, attractivity, invariance, closure, and compatibility
checks. N07 may discuss the RC theory of local computational irreducibility as
motivation for later agency work, but agency remains blocked. Identity
continuity is a prerequisite for later agentic-like integration, not an agency
claim.

N07 topology design is itself part of the experiment. The first identity
fixtures should be gate-isolating topology families, not rich omnibus scenes:
support-area minimal, stable-well basin, attractor-neighborhood, and
lineage-current invariance. Later reflexive closure and compatibility probes
should derive from those families so topology can explain which identity gate
passed or failed instead of becoming an authored label for the candidate.
After the primitive gates are legible, N07 should move to composite identity
ecologies: recurrent single-basin, lineage-current topology-mutating,
competing-basin compatibility, route-fed but route-independent, movement-carried
but movement-independent, and parent/child refinement boundary cases. These
composites should be classified by support/stability/attractivity/invariance/
lineage/closure/compatibility/replay gate vectors rather than by their topology
names.

## Producer Scaffolds And Native Absorption

N05-N11 may use LGRC producers as instrumented exploration scaffolds. This is
allowed when producers only observe committed runtime-visible state, emit
records, schedule through LGRC queues, and leave `step()` or committed topology
machinery as the mutation boundary.

Producer-mediated evidence is useful for discovering the mechanism shape:

```text
oscillation
-> route choice
-> identity / invariance
-> memory / trail
-> regulation
-> integration
```

It is not the final constitutive theory object. Every such result must record
whether the mechanism is:

```text
producer_mediated
threshold_authorized
native_route_arbitrated
constitutive_native
native_policy_gap
```

After N05-N11 produce enough evidence, run a retrospective Phase 8/native
absorption pass. That retrospective should ask:

```text
which producer decisions were essential?
which producer fields were only bookkeeping?
which thresholds should become serialized substrate policies?
which runtime-visible surfaces must be added to LGRC?
which claims remain invalid until producer logic is dissolved?
```

Do not prematurely add core LGRC mechanisms before the experiments identify the
minimal needed policy surface. Conversely, do not relabel producer-mediated
success as pure native dynamics. Runtime capability gaps should open Phase 8
work only when the experiment evidence shows exactly what needs to be absorbed
into LGRC proper.

Later non-ACO communication branches may compare:

```text
packet/pulse communication
flux-gradient communication
geometry or metric-deformation communication
trail/affordance memory
phase or oscillator synchronization
topology-event signaling
surface-lineage signaling
```

Those branches should be opened only when the current LGRC substrate evidence
makes their native mechanisms precise enough to define independent ladders and
controls.

## Agentic-Like Ladder

The A-ladder is a later integration ladder, not a synonym for producer
scheduling.

```text
A0:
    passive relaxation or externally scripted response

A1:
    state-triggered action from runtime-visible evidence

A2:
    native choice among alternatives

A3:
    memory-shaped choice through recorded trail/affordance state

A4:
    goal-proxy regulation of a runtime-visible condition

A5:
    identity-continuous regulation across cycles, perturbations, or topology
    changes

A6:
    bounded agentic-like integration: route choice, memory-shaped affordance,
    identity/support baseline, and goal-proxy regulation compose in one
    artifact-valid chain without hidden experiment-side steering

A7:
    broader/general agentic-like integration across changing contexts, support
    states, and proxy conditions, with artifact-only replay and explicit
    boundaries against agency, intention, and identity acceptance
```

N10 should target A5/A6 evidence. N11 should target A7 evidence if N10 closes
cleanly. Locomotion-like dynamics should be opened only after this integration
arc clarifies whether movement-like substrate behavior can be coupled to
identity/support and regulation without collapsing into agency or biological
claims.

## N11 Closeout Status

N11 closed the foundation arc at the intended artifact-only A7/GALI7 evidence
target:

```text
experiment = 2026-05-N11-lgrc-general-agentic-like-integration
final_supported_gali_ceiling = GALI7
final_claim_ceiling = broader_general_artifact_only_agentic_like_integration_candidate

```

This means the N05-N11 arc now has a source-backed artifact-only foundation for
broader/general agentic-like integration across declared route context, proxy
condition, support state, multi-axis matrix, longer-horizon, control, and
artifact-replay conditions.

This does not open the invalid implications listed below. In particular, N11
does not claim agency, intention, semantic goal ownership, identity acceptance,
RC identity collapse, ACO behavior, biological behavior, personhood,
unrestricted agency, or fully native LGRC agentic-like integration.

N11 also named the native absorption gaps behind the artifact-only foundation:

```text
native_route_conductance_memory_policy_missing
native_response_magnitude_policy_missing_for_unbounded_perturbations
native_identity_acceptance_validator_missing
native_agentic_like_integration_policy_missing
```

The concrete first native absorption candidates are route-conductance memory
and response-magnitude regulation, after preserving cross-cutting budget and
artifact-replay contracts. Identity acceptance remains theory-sensitive and
the native agentic-like integration meta-policy should come only after the
component policies are defined.
